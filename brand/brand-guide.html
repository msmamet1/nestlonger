Voice and Tone

Three words: Clear. Warm. Practical. Write for the adult child, not the aging parent. Short sentences. No jargon. Lead with the outcome, not the problem.

Do say

Your mom, your dad, your parent

Never say

Empowering families to...
Photography Direction
Real homes, not staged homes. Not Pottery Barn aging-in-place. Real kitchens, real bathrooms, real grab bars. Authenticity builds trust in this category.
Show adult children, not just seniors. The buyer is the adult child. Show them helping, on the phone, walking through a home with a parent. Not just an elderly person sitting in a chair.
Light and warmth, not clinical or alarming. No hospital imagery, no emergency rooms, no fallen person imagery. The emotional register is preparation and confidence, not fear.
Sources: Unsplash (search: aging, family home, parent), Getty authentic aging collection. Illustrations acceptable if photographic budget is zero.
