Version 1.0 - May 2026

## Positioning Statement

For adult children with aging parents who want to stay in their homes, NestLonger is the trusted guide that helps families get ahead of the problem - connecting them with certified professionals for the practical steps that make aging at home safe, affordable, and doable before the crisis forces their hand.

## Typography

### Headline Font: Libre Baskerville
- Google Fonts: `https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Libre+Baskerville:ital,wght@0,400;0,700;1,400`
- Use for: All H1, H2, H3, hero copy, pull quotes, taglines, eyebrow labels in serif

## Voice and Tone

Three words: **Clear. Warm. Practical.**

Write for the adult child, not the aging parent. Short sentences. No jargon. Lead with the outcome, not the problem.

### Do Say
- Your mom, your dad, your parent
- Aging at home / aging in place
- Certified installer / certified professional
- Before something happens / get ahead of it
- Here's what to do next
- Safe, doable, affordable
- Adult children / families

### Never Say
- Loved one
- Senior / elderly / aging adult
- End of life / final chapter
- When the worst happens
- Comprehensive solution / holistic / seamless / robust
- Empowering families to...
- Journey (overused)

---

## Photography Direction

1. **Real homes, not staged homes.** Real kitchens, real bathrooms, real grab bars. Authenticity builds trust.
2. **Show adult children, not just seniors.** The buyer is the adult child. Show them helping, on the phone, walking through a home with a parent.
3. **Light and warmth, not clinical or alarming.** No hospital imagery, no emergency rooms, no fallen person imagery. Emotional register: preparation and confidence, not fear.
4. **Sources:** Unsplash (search: aging, family home, parent), Getty authentic aging collection.

## What This Brand Is Not

- Not a tech startup (no purple gradients, no sans-serif-only, no "AI-powered" language)
- Not a medical provider (no clinical white, no hospital green, no caduceus imagery)
- Not a home improvement contractor (no tool imagery, no hardhat photos)
- Not a charity or nonprofit (no pity framing, no alarming statistics as the lead)

It is: a trusted, warm, practical guide. The most knowledgeable friend you have who happens to know exactly how to navigate this.
